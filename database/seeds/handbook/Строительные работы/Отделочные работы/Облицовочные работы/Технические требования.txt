<p class=MsoNormal id=SR5><span id=SB>- 100 мм - ±0,8;</span><span id=SS></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal id=SR5><span id=SB>- 150 мм - ±1,2;</span><span id=SS></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal id=SR5><span id=SB>- 200 мм - ±1,6;</span><span id=SS></span></p>
